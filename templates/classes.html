What We Teach

Curriculum Overview

Beginner (Ages 3–6)

Foundation
  • Navkar Mantra — introduction & recitation
  • Jain moral stories & values
  • Simple prayers and songs
  • Ahimsa — non-violence in daily life
  • Basic Jain symbols and their meaning

Level 1 (Ages 6–8)

Exploration
  • 24 Tirthankars — names and stories
  • Jain calendar and festivals
  • Five major vows (Pancha Mahavrata)
  • Ashtami & Chaturdashi observances
  • Introduction to Jain geography & places

Level 2 (Ages 8–12)

Understanding
  • Jain history and key figures
  • Nine Tattvas (fundamental principles)
  • Samayik — practice and meaning
  • Karma theory — introduction
  • Stories from Agam scriptures

Level 3+ (Ages 12+)

Deep Dive
  • Advanced Agam study (Uttaradhyayana, etc.)
  • Jain ethics and modern application
  • Six Substances (Shad Dravya)
  • Comparative philosophy
  • Debate and critical thinking in dharma

Adult Track (Ages 18+)

Spiritual Practice
  • Bhawna Yog — guided meditation and reflection
  • Tattvagyan — deep philosophical inquiry
  • Jain cosmology and metaphysics
  • Historical analysis of Agam texts
  • Community leadership and volunteer development
