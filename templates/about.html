Our Purpose

Our Mission

Global Jain Pramanik Pathshala (GJPP) exists to make authentic Jain education accessible to every Jain family on Earth — whether they're in Mumbai or Melbourne, London or Los Angeles.

Founded by a network of dedicated Jain volunteers, GJPP provides structured, level-based Jain education to students aged 3 through 99. We believe that cultural and spiritual heritage is a living inheritance — and that every child deserves to know the depth and beauty of Jain dharma.

Our platform combines online learning, local Pathshala discovery, volunteer empowerment, and community events — all driven by the values of Ahimsa, Satya, and Seva.
